The AND operator in SQL is used to combine multiple conditions in a query.
It returns records only when all conditions are TRUE.

✅ Basic Syntax

SELECT column_name
FROM table_name
WHERE condition1 AND condition2;


🔹 1. Simple AND Condition

SELECT * 
FROM Employees
WHERE Age > 25 AND City = 'Pune';

👉 Explanation:

Shows only employees:
Age greater than 25 AND
City is Pune
If one condition fails → row is NOT returned

🔹 2. AND with Multiple Conditions
SELECT * 
FROM Employees
WHERE Age > 25 AND Salary > 30000 AND City = 'Mumbai';

👉 Explanation:
All 3 conditions must be true:
Age > 25
Salary > 30000
City = Mumbai

🔹 3. AND with BETWEEN
SELECT * 
FROM Employees
WHERE Age BETWEEN 20 AND 30 AND Salary > 25000;

👉 Explanation:
Age must be between 20–30
AND salary must be greater than 25000

🔹 4. AND with LIKE
SELECT * 
FROM Employees
WHERE Name LIKE 'A%' AND City = 'Pune';

👉 Explanation:
Name starts with 'A'
AND city is Pune

🔹 5. AND with NOT
SELECT * 
FROM Employees
WHERE City = 'Pune' AND NOT Salary < 20000;

👉 Explanation:
City = Pune
AND salary is NOT less than 20000

🔹 6. AND with NULL
SELECT * 
FROM Employees
WHERE City = 'Pune' AND Email IS NOT NULL;

👉 Explanation:
City is Pune
AND email should not be empty

🔹 7. AND with UPDATE
UPDATE Employees
SET Salary = 50000
WHERE Department = 'IT' AND Experience > 5;

👉 Explanation:
Updates salary only for:
IT department
AND experience > 5

🔹 8. AND with DELETE
DELETE FROM Employees
WHERE City = 'Mumbai' AND Age < 22;

👉 Explanation:
Deletes employees:
From Mumbai
AND age less than 22

🔹 9. AND with JOIN
SELECT e.Name, d.DepartmentName
FROM Employees e
JOIN Departments d
ON e.DeptID = d.DeptID
WHERE d.Location = 'Pune' AND e.Salary > 30000;

👉 Explanation:
Join two tables

Filter:
Department in Pune
AND salary > 30000


🔹 Important Rule

👉 AND returns TRUE only when:

TRUE AND TRUE = TRUE
TRUE AND FALSE = FALSE
FALSE AND TRUE = FALSE
FALSE AND FALSE = FALSE

✅ Real-Life Example

Think like:

"I will go out if it is Sunday AND I have money"

Both must be true.

✅ Interview Answer
AND operator is used to combine multiple conditions in SQL, and it returns records only when all conditions are true.
